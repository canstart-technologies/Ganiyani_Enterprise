About Us

Our Journey

Who We Are

Crafting Excellence Since 2010

Ganiyani Enterprise began with a simple vision: to bring the world's finest ceramic craftsmanship to homes and businesses everywhere. What started as a small trading unit in Bhavnagar, Gujarat, has grown into a premier destination for premium tiles.

We believe that tiles are not just building materials; they are the canvas upon which life unfolds. From the quiet elegance of a morning kitchen to the grandeur of a corporate lobby, our surfaces set the tone for experiences that matter.

Milestones

Our History

2010

The Beginning

Founded in Bhavnagar, Gujarat, with a focus on local distribution of high-quality ceramic tiles.

2015

Expansion

Expanded our showroom capacity and introduced our first line of Porcelain tiles, setting new standards for variety.

2018

Global Reach

Started exporting to the Middle East and Africa, marking our entry into the international market.

2023

Innovation Hub

Launched our state-of-the-art R&D center to develop sustainable and large-format slab tiles.
